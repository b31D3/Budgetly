## What Was Fixed

**Before:** Summer income (`hoursSummer`) was completely ignored. The calculator only showed Fall and Spring semesters, both using school hours.

**After:** Summer periods are now properly included with higher work hours, showing the full financial picture.

---

## How It Works Now

### Timeline Structure (6 Semesters Example):

```
Fall 2026    ‚Üí Spring 2027 ‚Üí Summer 2027 ‚Üí
Fall 2027    ‚Üí Spring 2028 ‚Üí Summer 2028 ‚Üí
Fall 2028    ‚Üí Spring 2029 ‚Üí (No summer - graduation!)
```

### Calculation Per Period:

#### Fall/Spring Semesters:
```
Income:
- Work: school hours/week √ó 4 weeks √ó 4 months √ó rate
- Financial Aid: scholarship + bursary + grant
- Savings: total savings √∑ number of periods

Expenses:
- Academic: tuition + books + supplies (per semester)
- Living: monthly costs √ó 4 months
```

#### Summer Periods:
```
Income:
- Work: SUMMER hours/week √ó 4 weeks √ó 4 months √ó rate ‚úÖ
- Savings: total savings √∑ number of periods
- NO financial aid (typically not available in summer)

Expenses:
- Living: monthly costs √ó 4 months
- NO academic costs (no tuition in summer)
```

## Example Calculation

### Impact:

## Savings Distribution

Savings are now distributed across ALL periods (including summer):

**Example with $6,000 savings and 6 semesters:**
- Total periods: 6 semesters + 2 summers = 8 periods
- Savings per period: $6,000 √∑ 8 = **$750/period**

Each Fall, Spring, AND Summer gets $750 from savings.
